How to Place an Order

Follow these simple steps to get your goods shipped worldwide

Ready to Order
1

Click the Message Button

Look for the message button at the bottom right corner of your screen. This will open our direct communication channel.

2

Access Message Chat List

You'll be taken to a message chat list where you can contact our support team directly for personalized assistance.

3

Describe Your Goods

Provide detailed information about your products to our support team. Include dimensions, weight, and destination details.

4

Quick Booking Process

Your goods will be booked and processed in less than three hours. Our team works efficiently to get you started.

5

Payment & Tracking

After payment, you'll receive a tracking ID to track and follow up on your goods and services throughout the shipping process.
